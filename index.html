About Me

What's this all about?

I find too often that I get bored once I've tackled a task and look for ways to improve. One of the many reasons why I chose to dive into web development is that there is always a new and exciting way to make what I create better. Whether I'm refreshing my designs, organizing my code, or learning a whole new technology I find that the limit to what can be done in web development is endless.

I realized my interest in coding while taking electives in college. I found a database class that was challenging but also gratifying. Since then I've always wanted to make that feeling my career. I found that being able to make something was the feeling that made me happiest. My parents would remind me of my childhood and putting LEGOs together and making the cool pictures on the box, but I always remember trying to find cool and new ways to make something from what was in front of me.

What Else I Enjoy Doing

Besides spending all day in front of the computer making the cool web apps I also have a deep passion for ice hockey. I've been playing competitive hockey for 20 years and I follow my favorite team, the Florida Panthers, all season long. I can frequently been found in my home office with the hockey game on looking over an online documentation and trying to make something work or looking into a new design.

What drew me into hockey was the speed of the game and how difficult it is to master. Being able to skate on a 1/2" blade and move around on ice while making complex decisions about the game in the moment is beautiful. The physical and mental demands of the game are what give me the determination to go after new adventures and those long practices give me the patience to really perfect my crafts.

Other than sports I also really enjoy music, video games, and movie marathons with my girlfriend and our adorable Shiba Inu Scarlet.

My Work

Video-Idea-Saver

Video Idea Blog

Do you ever have an idea for something you'd like to see on Youtube or someother video site? Well with this Video Idea site you can create an account using an email and password and save ideas that you have created, edit, and delete any ideas. This app is on Version 1.0.0 so there will be updates along the way.

I've created this app using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Handlebars, Node, Express, and MongoDB.

Comic Shopper

Comic Shopper

Comics, Collectibles, Commerce. Findind that perfect variant of your favorite comic and those collectible you crave all together in this web app. Using secure Google OAuth I helped create this one stop shop for purchasing and saving your comics and collectibles in one place. You can search for a character or comic using our eBay API call and save these to your unique profile.

Once you've purchased that collectible you can add your certificate of authenticity to your profile so that you'll have that piece of mind that your statue or collectible is verified. This was a very fun and challenging project that will also be seeing updates as time permits.

This app was created using React, Node, Express, CSS3, Materialize, Mongoose, and MongoDB.
